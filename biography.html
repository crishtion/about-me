Hobbies and interests

  1. Running
      I am an avid runner, and often like to compete in any local runs that are going on. I love to push my body to its limits and see how much im capable of. I've completed a Spartan Trifecta(earned by completing a 5k, 10k and half marathon), which is one of my proudest achievements. I also hope to one day complete a marathon.
  2. Music
      Besides trumpet, which I play for my schools band, I enjoy playing other instruments, such as piano, guitar, and trombone. I also enjoy experimenting with a little bit of musical composition and have made a few small pieces just to enjoy.
  3. Film making & Acting
      I have directed a few films for both school and personal projects, and have even starred in a few for some of my classmates. It is a fairly new hobby of mine, and I have found it to be a fun, creative outlet. I have a youtube channel as well where I upload my films
  4. Coding and game making
      I also like to spend my free time learning how to code. I use many websites like freecodecamp, tryhackme, Codecademy and various tutorials on youtube
