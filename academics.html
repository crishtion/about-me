It’s no secret that the quality of most media has started to decline. I believe that there are multiple factors that have caused this downfall, but some of the greatest factors are the lack of passion and rising greed in studios and the development and implementation of new possibly harmful technologies.

    Firstly, I believe that one major reason the quality of the media industry has fallen, is because most media corporations put out their products purely for the sake of profit lacking passion. I feel that the market that has experienced this the most is the video game. The gaming industry has always been the topic of controversies and disregarded as a serious profession. As a result, people who did pursue this career had a sense of obligation to create games that could be enjoyed and taken seriously. In other words, people who worked at and ran studios were there because they were passionate about creating innovative mechanics, telling new stories and overall putting out quality releases. But as the industry grew and more revenue started to come in, larger corporations would buy out little studios just to treat them as a means to an end and put out games purely as cash grabs, completely throwing quality into the wind. Companies have stopped trying to innovate the industry. Rather opting to make formulaic games that revolve around microtransactions and cosmetics, that lack any real content despite pouring hundreds of millions of dollars into these projects. Some might argue that these poor products are results of time restraints and how expensive it is to make a game. I would like to mention that in recent years, time and again smaller game studios have performed and produced much more enjoyable content at a much cheaper production and sale price, than the AAA and AAAA studios.

    Furthermore, I feel that another reason the quality of the media industry has decreased is because of the experimentation and implementation of AI. With how rapidly AI has advanced there's lots of negatives that come with it. AI has proven that it can replace artists in all fields, some of the most concerning being with music and digital art. There are many AI models out there that can recreate human vocals, startlingly good. This use of AI has already made its way into mainstream music with rapper Kanye West making use of AI in his track “SKY CITY”. This was poorly received by many critics and his fan base, noting that it was very poorly implemented, creating distortions. AI has also made its way into the movie industry with studios looking for a way to save money. A24, a well respected and popular entertainment company recently faced backlash for the use of AI in their film “Late Night With the Devil”. While some might say that the use of I was minimal in the film and caused no harm to employees. I argue that AI has the potential to lead to another event similar to that of how automated machines replaced millions of employees in the automotive industry for the sake of increased productivity and decreased expense to upkeep. The truth is AI cant create art with emotion, the continued use of AI in media only contributes to the lack of passion.

    In summary, the media industry is plagued with innumerable problems. Most of which stem from the overbearing greed and new technologies such as AI. These issues have caused the downfall of many forms of media and left us in a “dark age” as consumers, where most products are poorly received and underwhelming. If these issues are not addressed and the corporations that create them are not checked, we can not expect to ever see an improvement in all forms of media.
